Love Banana are a five-piece art-pop group from the Gold Coast who bring a close artistic friendship and the enduring DIY indie spirit to their eclectic pop-rock style. The group's sound comes from a sphere of influences, traversing chuggy garage pop, scuzzy rock & roll and dusty folk territories, always with their playful, poppy touch.

The Last Few Years of Love Banana

From shows up and down the east coast to art shows in their kitchen, Love Banana has built a strong following over years of relentless touring. Collaboration drives everything they do. Songwriting is shared across members, and keyboardist/visual artist Paris Rodd shapes much of the band's artwork, videos and stage visuals.

Their recent self titled release saw #3 on the AIR Independent Singles chart, #10 on the Independent Label Singles chart, was named in Bandcamp Best Punk (Aug 2024), and premiered its video on New York Magazine – Hot Sounds. Tracks have also aired on 4ZZZ, PBS, Triple R, 2SER, RTRFM and Radio Elance in Spain.

They've shared stages with Babe Rainbow, Bananagun, Gee Tee, The Unknowns, RMFC and Dr Sure's Unusual Practice.

Love Banana continue to expand their artistic reach while staying grounded in their DIY beginnings — a band equally at home in galleries, backyards or big stages.
